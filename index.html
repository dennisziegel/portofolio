About Me

Data Analyst | Prompt Writer

My name is Dennis Jusuf Ziegel, but you can call me Dennis, Mr. Ziegel, or simply "Den" (never "Nis"). I am currently a student about to graduate with a degree in Information Systems. I am fluent in German, Indonesian, and English, and I have skills in Python programming, basic web development, prompt writing, and data analysis using EDA in Python.
